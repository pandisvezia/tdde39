Collaborative interaction
with physical-digital objects

A few questions on collaborative interaction

  • Does the physical set-up lead users to collaborate by subtly constraining their behavior?
  • Can all users see what is going on and get their hands on the central objects of interest?
  • Does the representation build on users’ experience? Does it connect with their skills and invite them into interaction?

Team project (Feb 14-Mar 21)

Design and prototype a physical-digital object concept informed by the knowledge built up in the design and coding sessions.

Team project

  • Find an idea, situation, or a topic around which you would like to design a physical-digital object
  • Sketch the initial idea(s) with code using Arduino and generate a few concepts
  • Select and detail one of the concepts, prototyping one or two core use cases or interaction sequences
  • Create a poster communicating your design concept and insights from the process

Deliverables

  • Poster communicating your design concept and insights from the process (group work)
  • Reflection paper where you talk about your experience and learnings from the exercises and the team project (individual work)
  • Submission date: Saturday, March 26
